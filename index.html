About Us

The Tanzania Cyber Academy is dedicated to providing top-tier cybersecurity education and training to protect digital assets and privacy.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and organizations with cybersecurity knowledge, skills, and best practices to combat cyber threats effectively.

Some of Contents/Courses you can Learn here:

At Tanzania Cyber Academy, we offer specialized courses in cybersecurity, including:

  • OSSEC - Host-Based Intrusion Detection: Learn how to use OSSEC for log analysis, file integrity monitoring, policy enforcement, and rootkit detection.
  • Security Onion - Threat Hunting Platform: Gain hands-on experience with Security Onion for threat hunting, network security monitoring, and log management.
  • Ethical Hacking: Understand penetration testing methodologies, exploit vulnerabilities, and secure systems effectively.
  • Digital Forensics: Learn how to investigate cybercrimes and analyze digital evidence.
  • Network Security: Master the principles of securing networks and preventing cyber attacks.
  • Malware Analysis: Study techniques to detect, analyze, and mitigate malware threats.
  • Cloud Security: Gain insights into securing cloud environments and managing risks.
  • Incident Response & Threat Intelligence: Learn how to detect, respond to, and mitigate security incidents.
  • IoT Security: Understand security challenges in IoT environments and learn best practices to protect devices.
  • Blockchain Security: Explore how blockchain technology can enhance cybersecurity and prevent fraud.
